About Me

Mariela Z Anderson has been working in application and web development for more than 25 years. Her formal career began in COBOL and JCL languages for IBM mainframe applications for New England Life Insurance. She pivoted to Web development and worked with Cold Fusion in the early days of the Web. After taking some time to raise three children she has returned to her love of coding and meeting the challenge of bringing new websites to life on behalf of clients large and small. Mariela revels in complex projects with multiple backend system integrations or simple WordPress brochure sites for small business and solopreneurs.
